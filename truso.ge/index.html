About Horse Riding Adventure in Truso Valley

Best time to visit: SPRING, SUMMER, AUTUMN

We are inviting you to our 4-hour horse riding tour in Truso Valley, which is heaven for nature lovers!

You will meet an abandoned village, ride along rivers, mountains, canyons, visit travertine mineral springs, a mineral lake, and enjoy wonderful fairy views during the whole ride!

The tour starts from Stepantsminda. We will proceed to Kobi, where our horses will be ready. You will be introduced to the rules of horse riding and start the tour with local horse guides.

After finishing the tour in Truso Valley, we will drive back to Stepantsminda.
